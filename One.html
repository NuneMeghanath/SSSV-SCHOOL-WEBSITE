Welcome to Sri Sai saisaraswathi Vidyanikethan link !

Sri Sai Saraswathi Vidyaniketan School is a co educational, English Medium school following CBSE syllabus till Grade VIII under the aegis of Sri Sai Saraswathi Vidyaniketan School Educational Society. Sri Sai Saraswathi Vidyaniketan School, in short, SSSVN School has been established in the year 2017 by Sri Bhavani Shankar, a philanthropist. Who believed in “school for all” where children of all categories are entitled for education. The school lays a strong foundation for the present and future of 21st century learners that includes life skills, emotional balance, ready to face challenges, and robust health. The school believes in values of being exhisting as a human being, highlighting integrity and ethics, and love for learning, as learning gives happiness. A happy mind is a receptive mind. Every child is encouraged to develop a strong, positive attitude towards self-development, that includes the 5C’S’-Creativity, Cooperation, Critical thinking, Communication and Confidence. Experiential learning is given importance. The school provides a strong foundation to train the students for life skills, emotional balance and to tackle challenging situations. SSSVN is now a landmark in Srinagar Colony Road, Yousufguda, a School gaining its popularity owing to the satisfied standards of holistic education.. The school is conveniently located catering to the needs of students and parents.!
